Our Programs

Three structured initiatives designed to bring institutional exposure directly to students across India.

Note on Access: All NYEI programs are offered free of cost to students and participating educational institutions. Programs are delivered in partnership with institutional heads and require formal coordination.
Program 01

Guest Lecture Series

The Guest Lecture Series brings credible, working professionals, researchers, and educators directly into the classroom — or the school hall. Each talk is structured around a student's most practical questions: What does a career actually look like? How did you get there? What should I be doing now?

Sessions are not motivational speeches. They are structured professional conversations, designed to give students a realistic and informed view of different career paths and institutional environments.

Program 02

Future Skills & AI Awareness Initiative

Artificial intelligence and digital tools are already changing every profession in India — from agriculture to law. But most schools have no structured way to introduce students to these changes. The Future Skills & AI Awareness Initiative fills that gap.

Sessions in this program focus on building students' awareness — not technical skills. We help them understand what AI is, how it is being used in different industries, and what kinds of skills will remain important regardless of automation.

This program is especially designed for students in Class 9 through Class 12 who are beginning to think about career choices in a rapidly changing world.

Program 03

Institutional Exposure Programs

Seeing is believing. There is a particular kind of learning that happens when a student steps inside a university, a government office, or a research facility for the first time. They don't just gain information — they gain a sense of what is possible for someone like them.

NYEI coordinates structured visits to institutions that are relevant to students' academic and career interests. These are not casual field trips. They are coordinated, agenda-driven visits with specific learning objectives, hosted by the partner institution.

Target institutions include universities, research labs, public sector bodies, and professional organizations that agree to host students under NYEI's coordination framework.
