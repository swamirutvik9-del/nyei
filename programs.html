Our Programs

Three active programs bringing real exposure to students across India. All programs are free.

All programs are free. NYEI does not charge students or institutions. Every session is coordinated directly with the school or college.
Program 01

Guest Lectures

NYEI organises guest lectures in engineering colleges and MBA institutions. We invite working professionals — engineers, entrepreneurs, managers, and domain experts — to speak directly with students.

These are not motivational talks. They are practical conversations where students can ask real questions and get honest answers about what professional life actually looks like.

Program 02

AI Awareness Workshops

Most students in rural and village schools have heard the word "AI" but have no idea what it actually means for their lives and future careers. This workshop changes that.

In simple, everyday language, we explain what artificial intelligence is, how it is already being used in India, and what skills students can start building today to stay relevant.

Program 03

Future Skills Sessions

Jobs are changing. Many of the skills that matter today were not taught in school a decade ago. Future Skills sessions help students understand what the world of work is moving toward — and how to prepare.

Topics include communication, digital literacy, problem-solving, and knowing how to learn on your own. These sessions are practical and interactive, not lecture-style.
